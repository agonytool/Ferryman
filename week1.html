Carpool concept itself have a lot of potential, both as a business idea and helps the environment.

Individual Work Area

    Research into 3 competitors Carpool businesses Take note of their functionalities

Understanding our Client

How many people is going to use the system? To better understand our customer base, we need to understand the different approaches to get information about the users.

The role of the user separates into the car provider and the passenger, and the mode of the transport which is the vehicle. Understanding the entities, we can then do statistical research to get numbers.

  • Help a cause that is environmentally friendly.

Looking at an ideal competitor like Grab, we can see which services they provide and their whole booking process, all the little things that can help build our system.

Comparison Chart

Finally compiling our individual research into a comparison chart of different functionalities between competitors within the market, summarise all of it and understand the key things.

Understanding our market, we will better know what kind of product we are engineering and how it should look.
