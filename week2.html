9:50pm - Creating a brief description in the App

We discuss everything that is to be done in-app including messaging services and application to be available to Apple and Android devices.

Reason

Exclusive to every students and staff of SIM using any device. Allow everyone to meet each other and knowing someone new. Reduce carbon footprint. Comfortable and faster way to get home as a lower cost. Our application prioritises safety and rest assured;you will be riding with people of the same community.

First Draft

Our platform is a carpool application that is available exclusively to students and staff of SIM using both android and apple devices. We care about the environment thus we promote one that reduces carbon footprint and traffic congestion. At the same time,lessen the financial burdens of the driver. Don't ever worry about not having a ride or squeezing with the evening crowd on public transport. Safety is our top priority,therefore, sit back and relax, knowing that you are getting home and reaching school with a member of the same community.

Company Tagline

Marketing it in a way to meet different students from other faculties. Lack of females in computer science major? Use our application to meet girls from art major.
